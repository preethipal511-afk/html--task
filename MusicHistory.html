MUSIC HISTORY
How Music Took Over the World


GLOBAL MUSIC EVOLUTION

Music began as a universal language of emotion and rhythm. Ancient civilizations used music for rituals, storytelling, and unity.

Over time, classical music brought structure, jazz and blues gave freedom, rock and pop created global youth culture, and technology helped music cross borders and languages.

With the rise of digital platforms, artists became worldwide icons. Music transformed into power, identity, and influence across cultures.


INDIAN MUSIC HISTORY

Indian music has a rich and spiritual history rooted in ancient traditions. It originated from the Vedas and evolved into classical forms like Hindustani and Carnatic music.

Indian classical music focuses on Raga and Tala, expressing deep emotions, devotion, and storytelling through sound.

Folk music reflects regional culture, festivals, and daily life, while film music brought Indian sounds to the global stage.

Today, Indian music blends tradition with modern genres like pop, fusion, and electronic music, influencing audiences worldwide.
